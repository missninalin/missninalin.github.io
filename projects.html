Projects and Experience

Most of the projects I did during my studies

SRI-RICE

I designed and developed the SRI-RICE ONLINE website, moving the website platform from Dreamweaver to Drupal, allowing everyone to contribute information easily to the website. This transition would bring the website into a more modern look, attracting more viewers.

ELEVATE

As part of the Hilton Hospitality Hackathon, I, with a team of 4 other "hackers", created a mobile application engraved into the Hilton Honors Program that would allow hotel stayers to experience a frictionless and personalized experience during their stay at the hotel and the local community.

BizBuzz

I, with a team of 5 other design students, came up with a website application that would allow business owners to communicate with other members of their business as well as communicate with local business owners and seek mentorship if needed.

Richard Yang Photography

I, with a team of 2 other coders, created a photography website for the client Richard Yang, who is starting out in photography and has a strong social media presence but no website. The website will be a platform for all his current and potential clients to view his work.

BreakFree

I, with a team of 5 other designers and coders, designed a website for the BreakFree club, a dance troupe on Cornell's campus. As the club did not have a website but did have strong social media presence, a website would congregate all their platforms together and give their users a unifying place to see all their performances.

Curology

As Curology does not currently have a mobile application, but has a strong website, I designed and developed a mobile application that aligned with Curology's current design themes and addressed problems given by current Curology patients.
