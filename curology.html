Type: Designing app for skincare company Curology
Role: UX/UI, Visual, Front-End
Timeline: June 2018- August 2018

Overview: Curology is a skincare company specializing in custom prescription strength acne treatment at an affordable price. Since its launch in 2014, Curology has gained huge momentum, standing out on social media and branding. However, Curology currently lacks an app and relies on its sole website to communicate with its customers. With the number of customers using Curology increasing, an app would provide a convenient and personalized place for customers to view/change their plans and track their progress. As an iintern for Curology during Summer 2018, I saw this need and decided to take on this challenge to build a mobile application.

Problem

Curology does not have a mobile application that allows customers to easily go onto their account and :

- Chat with their dermatology provider
- Track their progress
- Manage their treatment plan
- Engage with the Curology community, including other users

In order to achieve these tasks currently, users have to log in everytime at the Curology website.
