About Me

Hey there! I'm Nina Lin. I'm a New Jersey native, but I grew up in China so I often consider myself a mixture of east and west cultures. I currently study at Cornell University with a major in Information Science concentrated in User Experience. I plan to finish my undergraduate studies a year early before pursuing a Masters in Information Science. Besides from that, my goal in life to change the way people think and do things, making the impossible possible.

My Background

I grew up in a very tradition Chinese household, where my parents stressed education especically in the sciences and math. I grew up molding my life according to them, almost trying to fulfil their dreams and expectations. I've always had a passion for technology but felt it being suppressed by my parents' wish for me to become a doctor. It wasn't until my sophmore year in college that I wanted to chase my own dreams and do what I want to do in life. I greatly value family and appreciate everything my parents have done to raise me, and so everything I do, I do wholeheartedly as my goal is to make them proud.
