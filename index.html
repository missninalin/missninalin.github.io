About Me

Hi, I’m Nina Lin. I’m a User Experience Researcher and Designer. I like to spend my time discovering problems and solving them to make life simple and enjoyable. I like to make meaningful connections with people and help bond information, technology, and people together. I currently am a 3rd year student at Cornell University with an intended Bachelor's degree in Information Science with a concentration of User Experience. I love to conceptualize and design products. I would love to publicly call myself a “Maker” and you would find me tinkering with personal blog in my spare time. And as you are seeing this, I would like to connect with you.
